Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase artwork from Kala.ficial?

To purchase artwork, simply browse our collection, select the piece you love, and click "Add to Cart." Proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping information and payment details. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

How can I register as an artist on your platform?

To register as an artist, visit our Artist Portal and click on "Register." You'll need to provide some basic information, upload samples of your work, and our curation team will review your application. Once approved, you can start uploading and selling your artwork on our platform.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for all artwork. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us at returns@kala.ficial.com to initiate a return. The artwork must be returned in its original condition and packaging. Note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also view your order status by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

How are the artworks packaged for shipping?

We take great care in packaging artworks to ensure they arrive safely. Paintings are wrapped in glassine paper, cushioned with foam corner protectors, and placed in custom-sized boxes. Fragile items are double-boxed with additional cushioning. Canvas works are shipped without frames to prevent damage during transit.

Can I commission a custom artwork through Kala.ficial?

Yes, we offer custom commission services. Please visit our "Commission Art" page to submit a request. You'll need to provide details about your desired artwork including size, medium, style, and any reference images. Our team will match you with an appropriate artist and provide a quote.

How do exhibitions work on Kala.ficial?

We host both virtual and physical exhibitions. Virtual exhibitions can be accessed through our website, while physical exhibitions are held at partnered galleries. Tickets for physical exhibitions can be purchased through our website. Artists interested in exhibiting can apply through our Artist Portal.

What percentage does Kala.ficial take from artist sales?

We operate on a commission basis where artists receive 70% of the sale price, while Kala.ficial retains 30% to cover platform maintenance, marketing, payment processing, and customer service. There are no upfront fees for artists to join our platform.
